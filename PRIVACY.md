# Privacy Policy

## ChatGPT Archive Extension

**Effective Date:** March 1, 2026

### Overview

ChatGPT Archive is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

### Data Collection

**We collect ZERO personal data.**

This extension:
- ✅ Stores your ChatGPT conversations locally on your device only
- ✅ Uses AES-256 encryption with a PIN you choose
- ✅ Never sends your data to any external servers
- ✅ Never tracks your usage or behavior
- ✅ Never uses analytics or telemetry

### Data Storage

All data is stored:
- **Location:** Your browser's local storage only
- **Encryption:** AES-256 with your PIN-derived key
- **Access:** Only you, with your PIN
- **Transmission:** None - no network requests with your data

### Permissions

We request these permissions solely for functionality:

- **activeTab:** To access ChatGPT page content for backup
- **storage:** To store your encrypted archive locally
- **downloads:** To export your conversations to files
- **sidePanel:** To provide a persistent archive viewer

### Third-Party Services

Optional integrations (only when you explicitly choose to export):
- Notion API (when you export to Notion)
- GitHub API (when you create a Gist)
- These services receive data only when you initiate an export
